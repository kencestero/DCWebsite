Who We Are

Diamond Cargo was established in 2007 by Jerry Waldron and has grown into one of the most trusted enclosed cargo trailer manufacturers in North America. We're a family-owned operation conveniently located about 50 miles east of I-75 in Douglas, Georgia—the cargo trailer hub of America.

We manufacture enclosed cargo trailers exclusively for licensed dealerships. This B2B model allows us to focus entirely on what we do best: building premium, customizable trailers with unmatched quality and reliability.
